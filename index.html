Psychology Scholarship deadline approaching

Are you thinking about a career change but concerned about the cost?

At The University of Law, we understand that a Master's course can be a challenging financial commitment and that's why our Career Changer Scholarship is available to support you with your fees and help you invest in your future. We have both full fee and partial fee scholarships available for those wishing to study our MSc Psychology (Conversion) course this February.

The deadline for applications is Sunday 12 November. Don't miss out, visit our website for more information and eligibility criteria.
